How To Play

The game uses a 3x3 grid and needs two players: one as "X" and the other as "O." The game begins with an empty grid, and players take turns placing their symbol in one of the nine available spaces.

The objective is to be the first player to align three of your symbols in a straight line, which can occur horizontally, vertically, or diagonally across the grid. As each player takes their turn, they should aim to block their opponent from achieving three in a row while trying to complete their own line of three.

The game concludes when one player successfully aligns three of their symbols, or if all nine spaces are filled without completing a line, resulting in a draw.

Game Modes

Step into the sleek and immersive world of Tic Tac Toe, where you can choose between two exciting game modes.

In "Play with a Person", challenge a friend or a family member and engage in a face-to-face battle of wits, skill, and strategy. Feel the tension rise with each turn as you try to outsmart your opponent.

Alternatively, test your skills against the AI in "Play with Computer" mode. This mode offers a unique experience, pitting you against a cunning virtual opponent with unpredictable strategies. Each victory is a testament to your tactical prowess.

Choose your mode, sharpen your mind, and let the game begin!
